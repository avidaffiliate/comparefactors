Get Quotes from Property Factors

Tell us about your property and we'll connect you with registered factors serving your area. Free, no-obligation quotes.

Why use our service?

✓
Only registered factors. Every factor we work with is on the Scottish Property Factor Register—it's a legal requirement.
✓
We've done the research. We check tribunal records, reviews, and company data so you can compare factors with confidence.
✓
No pressure, no obligation. Factors quote based on your requirements. You're under no obligation to proceed with any of them.
✓
Completely free. Our service costs you nothing. Factors pay us a small fee when they receive your details.
