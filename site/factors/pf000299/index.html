How do I make a complaint about Stirling Council?
First, raise your complaint directly with Stirling Council using their formal complaints procedure (required by law to be in their Written Statement of Services). If unresolved after 8 weeks, you can apply to the Housing & Property Chamber. Visit housingandpropertychamber.scot to start an application.
