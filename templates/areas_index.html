About Property Factors in Scotland

Property factors (also known as property managers) are responsible for maintaining common areas in flatted developments across Scotland. All factors must be registered on the Scottish Property Factor Register and comply with the Property Factors Code of Conduct.

Use Compare Factors Scotland to find factors operating in your area, view their tribunal records, and read customer reviews before making a decision.
